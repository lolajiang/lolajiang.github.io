Redesigning AI training for everyone

I was the sole web designer for a leading conversational AI training platform for bank accounts. Making AI training make sense to people, so they can train AI easily.

Abstract

Problems
  • Poor information architecture and fuzzy training process led to horrendous usability issues.
  • Lackluster visual style and unclear visual hierarchy frustrated users.
Solutions
  • Offered two extra pages (an overview page & a training page) to imrpove efficiency of use, inform users of system status, and walk users through the training process.
  • Built a new design system to make visual and experience improvements across the training platform.
Results
  • With the original design, the set of tasks took 19 minutes. With the new design, the set of tasks took 6 minutes. The improvement is 217%.
  • Users' subjective satisfaction with the new design (4.3/5) was 139% higher than the original design (1.8/5).
My Role

Conduct user research, discover usability issues, identify key use cases, improve information architecture, revamp visual style, and code the real thing.

Background

Clinc builds the most advanced conversational AI platform for financial services, and delivering enterprise grade AI solutions to the world's leading banking and financial industry.

As the only designer working on Clinc's AI training platform, I led the project to revamp the visual style and solve the key usability issues by continuously re-imagining what it’s like for Clinc's users to train AI. I'll share my learning on how I understand user’s mental model and break down the barriers to help them train AI easily.

Problem Space

Newbies are lost due to fuzzy training process and poor information architecture

Let's start with a story.

Imagine you are a newbie to AI training. You want to build 3 AI robots, so you order all the materials you need online. However, when you receive the backages, you come across several problems:

  • There's no handbook at all. So you have no idea what to do next.
  • You are bombarded with the confusing terminologies.
  • The materials are mixed by 4 boxes. How to pick all the materials for a specific AI robot out of those boxes?

You end up being stuck in the middle, confronted with an error, with no confidence to go through the whole daunting process.

Experts are stuggling with inefficiency of use and low visibility of system status

Wait. What if you are an expert?

Now you should be more confident because you know what exactly the AI training process is. However, you are also struggling with building the AI robots because:

  • You have to constantly shifting between 4 boxes to search among the mixed materials. It's so cumbersome.
  • You have no idea whether each step works well or not.
  • You mess everything up because you lose the track of each individual training progress. (Remember you have to train 3 AI robots at once.)

You waste tons of hours because you have to keep checking if you have choosed the right materials out of the right box for the right AI robot.

Original information architecture.
Imagine how cumbersome it is to shift between 4 boxes to pick all the materials for a specific AI robot.

New design imrpoves efficiency of use, informs users of system status, and walks users through the training process

Now introducing the new design.

This time you receive 3 boxes, each dedicated to a specific AI robot. Accompanied with the boxes is the training handbook. You are much more confident to do the training because:

  • You know exactly what to do with the help of handbook.
  • You are more confident that you are doing changes on the right AI robot. No need to shifting between 4 boxes any more.
  • You can have detailed control over training steps. No confusion on whether it works at all.
New information architecture.
See how easy it is to work on a specific AI robot with a box full of all the relevant materials.

Design Decisions

Welcome back to the design world!

The problems users face while using our website are quite similar with the lovely characters. Users can't rely on the navigation bar to walk through the training process. Just imagine how many problems the 4 boxes in the first two sad stories have caused. So,

  • How to make sense of the complex system?
  • How to communicate the training process to people?
  • How to help people gather key info without getting lost?

Overview Page empowers users to keep everything on track with better information architecture

Mirroring the solutions in the previous story, I also decided to offer an overview page with all the cards, each of which dedicated to a specific AI competency. This means:

  • You can see all the related info at a glance without digging deep into the website. No need to shifting between other pages any more.
  • You can have detailed control over training steps. You are informed of the states of training and what to do next.
New Overview Page. Users can keep track of the training progress easily.

Training Page empowers users to train AI at their fingertips without burden of switching pages

If you hit the button called "Go to Training Guide", you will land on the training page with the following benefits:

  • You know exactly what to do with the help of handbook.
  • You are more confident that you are doing changes on the right AI competency.
  • You can do the training directly in the work area. No need to jump back and forth between other pages any more.
Training Guide with Work Area. Users can train AI at their fingertips.

Results

All of the changes I made were received positively from users. I also did two usability testings to gauge the effectiveness of the new design.

  • With the original design, the set of tasks took 19 minutes. With the new design, the set of tasks took 6 minutes. The improvement is 217%.
  • Users' subjective satisfaction with the new design (4.3/5) was 139% higher than the original design (1.8/5).

Design System

For the design system, I wanted to mature the Clinc brand and take the visual style from something that was very bootstrap like to incorporating more vivid elements and mirroring the visual style of our main website.

After I created a basic style guide, I began mockup up the screens in code by creating new stylesheets. Design by coding made it really easy for me to show in real-time what projects might look like to stakeholders. This approached was much faster and allowed for quick iteration.

An accurate illustration of the AI training process. Iterated with the help of AI engineers.

Challenges

The biggest challenge for this project was navigating the problem space. The task of “building a design system” sounded so daunting at first compared to how comprehensive and complicated the iOS design system or the Google Material Design system are. I am glad my design hit the bar.

AI training itself is also a new problem space for me. I had to keep bugging my colleagues to understand how it actually works. With this said, I got the chance to work with so many amazingly talented individuals before I left, so it was definitely worth it.
